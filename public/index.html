About DormLink

A Smarter Way to Find a Boarding House

DormLink simplifies the process of finding the right boarding house. No middlemen, no confusing listings — just clear, honest information from verified landlords directly to you.

  • Browse listings without signing up — no account needed
  • See all amenities, house rules, and availability at a glance
  • Contact the landlord directly using the listed number
  • Listings always up-to-date — managed directly by the owner
