## 📌 Project Description

**DormLink: Smart Boarding House System** is a web-based platform designed to simplify the process of finding and managing boarding house accommodations. The system connects students looking for places to stay with landlords who want to advertise and manage their boarding house listings.

The system provides students with organized access to boarding house information such as rental prices, room availability, amenities, house rules, and landlord contact details. Meanwhile, landlords can manage property listings through a secure dashboard where they can add, update, and remove boarding house information.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

The main objective of DormLink is to develop a centralized web-based system that improves the process of searching and managing boarding house information.

Specifically, the system aims to:

- Provide students with an easier way to search for available boarding houses.
- Allow landlords to efficiently manage and advertise their properties.
- Organize boarding house information in a centralized database.
- Improve accessibility and convenience in finding suitable accommodations.
